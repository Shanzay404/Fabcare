Qualities of good caregiver

1. Ability to understand patient's feelings

Having a personal understanding of and connection to the patient's situation is crucial for a caregiver to provide the best care possible.

2. Should have patience

In the caring field, patience is a skill that everyone must have.

3. Should be passionate

Caregivers are effective at their jobs because they approach it with passion.

4. Should be attentive

A patient needs ongoing care and supervision, especially if they are elderly. Therefore, even if the patient is unable to verbalise those demands, it is the caregiver's responsibility to be aware of them.

5. Trustworthiness

The nature of their work frequently allows caregivers access to the possessions of the person they are looking after. It is crucial that the caregiver be someone who won't betray that trust by mistreating the person they are caring for.
